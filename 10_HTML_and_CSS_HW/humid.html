Summary: Latitude vs. Humidity

As displayed in the figure above, there appears to be very little correlation between latitude and humidity. There are cities with high humidity at nearly every latitude while low humidity is also spread uniformly throughout the figure. There is one noticable absence of low humidity values between 0 and -20 degrees latitude. No other patterns appear to be visible.
